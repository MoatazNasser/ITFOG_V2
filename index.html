Tunnels

ITFOG delivers advanced High Fog (Water Mist) fire suppression systems designed specifically for tunnel protection. Our technology rapidly controls fire, heat, and smoke at the source while using minimal water. By blocking radiant heat, ITFOG High Fog systems help preserve tunnel structures and improve conditions for safe evacuation and firefighting. The system can be activated instantly—manually or automatically—through integrated fire detection, ensuring fast response, enhanced life safety, and reduced tunnel downtime.

Rapid fire suppression directly at the fire source
Significant reduction of heat and smoke spread
Minimal water usage compared to conventional deluge systems
Protection of tunnel structural integrity through radiant heat blocking

Low maintenance, high reliability, and long service life
